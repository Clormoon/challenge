Rules of Catch N' Run © playtime Games Studio

There are 3 sides: orc, elf, and dwarf. each side can only hunt one other side and is only hunted by one other side

In each game, there is only 1 human joe per team (unless 2v2v2 or 3v3v3) and lots of neutral bots on the map. At the beginning the goal is to hit the neutral bots to convert them into your team.
Then there are 2 ways to win: either at the end of the time limit (10 min) the one who has the most people in his team (not captured) wins
